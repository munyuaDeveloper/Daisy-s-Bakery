Chocolate Chip Muffins

Don’t think you need to spend hours slaving over a hot oven to make a delicious sweet to share. This slice is a bit of a modern-day Aussie classic and makes a great treat to share over afternoon tea. The glacé cherries are super juicy, while the coconut and condensed milk make the perfect chewy centre. The whole lot is sandwiched between a lovely biscuit base and a creamy chocolate top. Best enjoyed with a nice cup of tea!
