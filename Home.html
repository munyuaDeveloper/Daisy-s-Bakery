Welcome

We are one of the few bakeries in Townsville that make and bake everything by hand, Producing all types of bakery products including pies and pasties, muffins, assorted slices, large variety of breads and tortes, cheesecakes as well as special occasion cakes. We specialize in German based continental cakes, pastries and bread.
As well as the traditional Aussie affair. We are proud to say that we only use the finest and freshest local and imported German products.
