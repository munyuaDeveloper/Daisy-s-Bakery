Welcome to Daisy's Bakery

Hello there, everything began with my enthusiasm for cooking when I was a young lady. I have delighted in cooking and preparing for my family since I was 8 years of age.
I started my working career as a secretary and an office director in Brisbane. However, I generally had my brain on cooking. Cooking for my husband, cooking for my family. Then I started taking cooking classes at night.
Before I moved to the Townsville to rejoin with my family, with my husband's help, I went to Switzerland to seek after Hotel Management at Les Roches, and was granted a Diploma in Swiss Hotel Management in 1993. In addition to my studies in Switzerland, I trained expertly at the world popular Le Cordon Bleu in Paris, where I got a recognition spent significant time in French Cuisine and Pastries.
With the encounters at these five-star inns, I built up a range of skills to make cakes with exquisite taste that have turned into the establishment for Daisy's Tropical Treats Bakery. Some of my current customers are James Cook University, the Cowboys and Telstra.
